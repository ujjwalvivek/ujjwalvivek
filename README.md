#### Hi, I’m Vivek. 

I speak fluent Engineer. I understand the difference between `O(n)` and `O(n^2)` and why we can't *just add networking* over the weekend. And I treat every product as `systems` and optimizing them for latency, scalability, and user-trust **(Privacy-First/Local-First)**.

I bridge the gap between *Business Requirements* and *Technical Constraints*. **I’ve spent the last few years building 0→1 products, gaming platforms, scaling enterprise VR simulations to 65,000+ users, launching 5 MVPs in 6 months doubling engagement metrics, and optimizing logistics operations to cut fulfillment latency by 50%**.

If you are looking for a TPM who is comfortable reading API docs and challenging architectural decisions, let’s talk.
